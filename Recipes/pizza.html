Pizza

A favourite evening snack

Pizza is a beloved Italian dish that has become a global favorite. It typically consists of a thin crust topped with tomato sauce, melted cheese, and a variety of toppings such as vegetables, meats, and herbs. The combination of savory flavors and crispy texture makes pizza a delicious and versatile meal that can be enjoyed any time of day.

Ingredients

  • 1 pre-made pizza crust
  • 1/2 cup pizza sauce
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Optional toppings: sliced mushrooms, bell peppers, onions, pepperoni, or other toppings of your choice

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 425Â°F.
  2. Place the pre-made pizza crust on a baking sheet or pizza stone.
  3. Spread pizza sauce evenly over the crust, leaving a small border around the edges.
  4. Sprinkle shredded mozzarella cheese over the sauce, covering the entire surface of the pizza.
  5. Add any desired toppings.
  6. Bake for 10-12 minutes or until the cheese is melted and the crust is golden brown.
  7. Slice and serve hot.
    8. Enjoy your delicious homemade pizza!
